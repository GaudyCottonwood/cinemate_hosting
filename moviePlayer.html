Movie Title

"Her" is a 2013 science fiction romantic drama film directed by Spike Jonze. The story is set in a near-future Los Angeles and follows Theodore Twombly (played by Joaquin Phoenix), a lonely, introverted man who writes personal letters for a living. Recently separated from his wife, Theodore forms an unlikely relationship with an advanced artificial intelligence operating system named Samantha (voiced by Scarlett Johansson). As their bond deepens, Theodore grapples with the complexities of love and human connection in an increasingly digital world. The film explores themes of intimacy, identity, and the impact of technology on personal relationships, offering a poignant and thought-provoking look at the nature of love in the modern age.
